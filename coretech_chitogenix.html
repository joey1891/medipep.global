RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Multifunctional Hybrid Platform

Chitogenix™ is a multifunctional hybrid platform that integrates a physiologically responsive hydrogel system based on water-soluble chitosan (CHITOGEN™) with a biological signal delivery platform based on decellularized extracellular matrix (REGENTRIX™).

Mechanism of Action: STEP 1

Preparation of regenerative condition – CHITOGEN™: Based on LTG (Liquid-to-Gel) technology, forming a stable hydrogel matrix that stabilizes under physiological conditions. It establishes a tissue-friendly microenvironment that maximizes cellular responsiveness.

Mechanism of Action: STEP 2

Signal Recognition & Communication – REGENTRIX™: Manufactured through supercritical fluid processing and low-molecular structuring. It functions not as a passive material, but as a biological signaling platform recognizable at the cellular receptor level.

Paradigm Shift in Regeneration Design

Chitogenix™ represents a shift from 'what to add' (Conventional) toward 'how to design the conditions and biological language' (Chitogenix™) through which tissue responds. It enables predictable, balanced, and self-regulated tissue responses.

Molecular Synergy & Regeneration Proof

Question 01

How does CHITOGENIX™ establish a regenerative microenvironment?

The platform utilizes the unique polycationic nature of Chitosan. Its primary amine groups ($-NH_2$) become protonated into $\text{-NH}_3^+$ in physiological environments ($pK_a \approx 6.3\text{--}6.5$).

This positive charge creates electrostatic attraction with the negatively charged phospholipids of mammalian cell membranes, significantly promoting cell adhesion, proliferation, and differentiation.

Question 02

What "biological signals" are delivered by the dECM component?

REGENTRIX™ (dECM) delivers a "cocktail" of synergistic proteins and growth factors that act as molecular work instructions for the cells.

Angiogenesis (VEGF)

Binds to $\text{VEGFR}$ to restore blood supply and nutrient delivery.

Proliferation (bFGF)

Induces fibroblast activity and commands new ECM synthesis.

Remodeling ($\text{TGF-}\beta$)

Promotes collagen synthesis via the $\text{SMAD}$ signaling pathway.

Epithelialization (HGF)

Stimulates epithelial cell migration while inhibiting scar formation.

Question 03

Why is the hybrid combination of Chitosan and dECM necessary?

The synergy is based on a mutually complementary mechanism:

  • Stabilization: Chitosan forms a "nano-reservoir" around growth factors, protecting them from enzymatic inactivation and extending their half-life dozens of times.
  • Mechanical Reinforcement: Chitosan strengthens the physical skeleton of the dECM hydrogel through chain entanglement, preventing it from being washed away in clinical settings.
  • Timed Release: As the complex degrades at a rate synchronized with tissue regeneration, it ensures a sustained and controlled release of signaling proteins.
