RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Multifunctional Hybrid Platform

Chitogenix™ is a multifunctional hybrid platform that integrates a physiologically responsive hydrogel system based on water-soluble chitosan (CHITOGEN™) with a biological signal delivery platform based on decellularized extracellular matrix (REGENTRIX™).

Mechanism of Action: STEP 1

Preparation of regenerative condition – CHITOGEN™: Based on LTG (Liquid-to-Gel) technology, forming a stable hydrogel matrix that stabilizes under physiological conditions. It establishes a tissue-friendly microenvironment that maximizes cellular responsiveness.

Mechanism of Action: STEP 2

Signal Recognition & Communication – REGENTRIX™: Manufactured through supercritical fluid processing and low-molecular structuring. It functions not as a passive material, but as a biological signaling platform recognizable at the cellular receptor level.

Paradigm Shift in Regeneration Design

Chitogenix™ represents a shift from 'what to add' (Conventional) toward 'how to design the conditions and biological language' (Chitogenix™) through which tissue responds. It enables predictable, balanced, and self-regulated tissue responses.
