ROOT CAUSE
MICROENVIRONMENT

Resnovae ScalpMD® shifts the paradigm of hair loss treatment from the follicle itself to the surrounding microenvironment. By addressing chronic inflammation, oxidative stress, and ECM collapse, it restores the biological soil necessary for healthy hair growth.

CORE TECHNOLOGY: CHITOGENIX™

LTG (Liquid-to-Gel) Chitosan

A patented water-soluble chitosan that transforms into a stable gel upon contact with the scalp, ensuring suppression of TNF-α and establishing a regenerative barrier.

Purified Sc-sECM

Incorporates purified decellularized extracellular matrix to rebuild the collapsed dermal matrix around hair follicles, providing vital signaling proteins.

MECHANISM OF ACTION

TNF-α Suppression

Directly inhibits inflammatory cytokines to prevent follicular degradation and chronic scalp irritation.

ROS Management

Neutralizes Reactive Oxygen Species (ROS) that trigger hair thinning and premature aging of the scalp tissue.

ECM Reconstruction

Rebuilds the extracellular matrix, providing physical support and a healthy foundation for new growth.
