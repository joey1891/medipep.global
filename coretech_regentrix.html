RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Next-gen Decellularized Matrix

Regentrix™ utilizes tissue-derived extracellular matrix (dECM) to provide an optimal microenvironment and cell signaling for tissue regeneration, surpassing the limitations of synthetic scaffolds.

Supercritical CO2 Process

Developed a proprietary method for preparing biocompatible decellularized matrices using supercritical carbon dioxide, ensuring high safety and preservation of ingredients and structural proteins.

Water-soluble dECM Innovation

Breakthrough in creating water-soluble decellularized matrices, enabling flexible application in injectable and topical regenerative therapies.

Immune Regulation & Cellular Signal Transduction

Rather than a simple immune response-driven boosting, it achieves fundamental tissue regeneration by promoting multi-mechanistic cell signaling through its complex components.
