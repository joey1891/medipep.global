RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Limitations of Existing Fillers & Objectives

Existing Hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers require high injection pressure and risk granuloma formation. Our objective was to develop a next-generation physiological ion-responsive carboxymethylchitosan filler platform.

Core Manufacturing Technology

Neutral water solubility and physiological ion-responsive phase transition (Liquid-to-Gel) is achieved by the integration of specific chemical structures in carboxymethylchitosan with phosphate ions.

Key Performance: 4.7x Lower Injection Force

Carboxymethylchitosan filler demonstrates an injection force approximately 4.7 times lower than HA fillers using 30G needles, ensuring precise and easy application.

Longevity & Safety

Chitogen spreads quickly throughout skin tissues after injection and provides a longer-lasting effect compared to conventional Polynucleotide (PN) fillers, ensuring superior clinical durability.

Key Feature

CHITOGEN™ acts as a smart scaffold that responds to body temperature and ions, transforming from a liquid to a firm gel once injected, providing immediate structural support.

Application

Primarily used in medical aesthetics as a dermal filler base and in tissue engineering applications where a bio-responsive matrix is required.
